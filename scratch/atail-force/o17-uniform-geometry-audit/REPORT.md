## Bottom line

There are three different closure questions and they must not be conflated.

1. **Bounded six-center residual elimination.**  PG148--PG151 and
   Propositions 196--199 remove the remaining `000112` records.  Lemma 200 /
   PG152 reduces each of the two `000111` records to sixteen A/B type words.
   The resulting 32 words still require front, endpoint, and linked
   metric--angle elimination.  Even eliminating all 32 would close only the
   six-center strong-middle classification.

2. **Arbitrary mixed/dense sink closure.**  This is O17.  It is still open.
   Propositions 189--190 give an explicit unbounded abstract `E = 3` family,
   even with the full arbitrary-selection strong-connectivity condition.
   Therefore critical cover, selected-row strong connectivity, blocker
   cycles, and current pair capacities cannot prove O17 without additional
   Euclidean order, angle, radius, MEC/cap, or critical-deletion geometry.

3. **Actual source-sorry implication.**  O17 is only a prose-proof frontier.
   O18 and O19 are also explicitly `UNPROVEN`.  A Lean source `sorry` closes
   only after a cardinality-independent geometric theorem is formalized on
   the actual parent surface, consumed to `False`, combined with the
   protected unique-radius arms, and wired before the LIVE split.  None of
   the bounded six-center results performs that implication.

Accordingly, this audit found no new result that can honestly be described as
closing an actual `sorry`.

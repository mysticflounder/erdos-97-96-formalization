1. mutual source cross membership and a common blocker;

For the natural four-point role assignment

```text

```

the production frontier theorems rule out both ways of completing the local
branch:

- if `pair.w` is in `selectedAt(pair.q).support`, the second equidistance is
  available, but `signedArea_product_neg_of_cross_membership` proves the two
  source points lie on opposite sides of the center chord.  Hence the
  forbidden cyclic order `q,u,y,v` cannot hold;
- if the cross deletion survives, then
  `blocker_dist_ne_of_cross_deletion_survives` proves the two distances from
  that blocker are unequal, so the second equality-closure relation is

forces the blockers to coincide; a one-way branch gives only one source-pair
equality; reciprocal nonmembership gives neither.  Thus the current local
frontier not only fails to produce the compact four-point core: its own

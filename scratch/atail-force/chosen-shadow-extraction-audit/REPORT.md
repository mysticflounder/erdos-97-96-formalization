regression surfaces. They are **not** the codomain of a currently plausible

2. construction of both full exact apex filters with the hard-coded sizes and
   their relation to the selected frontier rows; and

The first family is an unavoidable mismatch: the live theorem has no upper
cardinality bound, while the surface contains only `11:(5,5,4)` and
`12:(6,5,4)/(5,5,5)`. No amount of additional bank coverage on that surface
can prove a uniform parent theorem.

Therefore the go/no-go gate **fails for the fixed surface**. Keep its existing
certificates and censuses, but do not extend its CEGAR stream as the primary
closure route and do not implement a live extractor into it.

The narrower route remains admissible: choose the actual parent witnesses
`j, S, Hinitial, r, F, K, P` dependently, then test whether one checked

## Why these gaps are independent

The fixed profile can be supplied without any critical-row provenance: that
is exactly what the current finite surface does. Conversely, a total critical
shell system and global K4 can exist on carriers outside the fixed profiles,
as the saved regression models demonstrate. Neither family reconstructs the
other.

Likewise, bank membership is downstream of an already constructed finite
shadow. A complete classifier cannot choose the live labels, prove a full
filter has size at most five, or identify a critical source with one of its
roles. Increasing the number of bank consumers cannot repair an absent
extraction map.

This means the open content cannot honestly be summarized as one theorem of
the form “the full geometry chooses the right bank arm” on the present fixed
surface.
